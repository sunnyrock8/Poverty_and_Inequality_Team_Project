Solutions

We plan on achieving our goal, which is to use uproot the problem of poverty, using education. The lack of education leads difficulties in employment, which eventually leads to poverty. Such people that are not well educated and have low incomes also face inequality in society. By donating to NGO’s we can help them with more funds because of which they will get motivated to help many more individuals.

The other way poverty can be reduced is if the government starts charging higher taxes from the rich and uses the same for poor people’s education, this will solve the problem of inequality, and will redistribute income more evenly. Also the government should provide free medical treatment for the poor as most of their earnings are used in daily life, they might not have enough for medical emergencies.
